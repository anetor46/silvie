# Silvie — AI Personal Assistant for teams that travel

**Silvie** is an AI-powered personal assistant for business teams that travel for work — sales reps, consultants, field engineers, traveling managers, founders, and customer success teams. These are professionals who burn hours every week on the same kind of administrative work a personal assistant would normally handle, but who almost never get one assigned to them.

Unlike traditional travel apps, calendar tools, or expense platforms, Silvie is not focused on one narrow task. Instead, it gives every team member their own AI assistant that quietly handles the operational complexity of being on the road: schedules, travel, meetings, bookings, logistics, receipts, and follow-ups.

Silvie connects to each user’s email, calendar, travel platforms, expense tools, taxi apps, restaurant booking systems, and CRM where relevant. It automatically detects and organizes flights, hotels, train tickets, restaurant reservations, taxis, meetings, invoices, receipts, and missing actions into one clear mobile-first dashboard.

The core value of Silvie is not simply booking a flight or scheduling a meeting. Its real value is in keeping track of everything that has already been booked, what still needs to be done, what has changed, and what may cause problems. Silvie helps users avoid missed meetings, poor travel planning, missing invoices, forgotten receipts, calendar conflicts, and last-minute logistics issues.

For example, Silvie can identify that a flight has been booked but no hotel is attached to the trip, that a restaurant reservation has not been added to the calendar, that a taxi is needed after landing, or that a customer meeting is too close to the arrival time. It can also enrich calendar events with addresses, travel time, confirmation numbers, booking links, receipts, and relevant notes.

Silvie is best understood as **the AI personal assistant your team never had**. It sits above existing tools rather than replacing them. Instead of competing directly with platforms like Concur, Navan, TravelPerk, Outlook, Google Calendar, Uber, or OpenTable, Silvie integrates with them and gives each team member a unified, intelligent layer across all of their commitments.

The product is especially valuable for the kinds of teams that travel constantly but historically have not had personal assistants: outside sales, consulting and advisory teams, field service, customer success, account management, and the growing population of road-warrior individual contributors at scale-ups. These users often have complex days involving flights, hotels, client meetings, internal calls, dinners, taxis, receipts, and follow-up actions. Silvie helps them stay in control without needing a human personal assistant — and gives their employer a way to roll out that capability across an entire team rather than only at the very top of the org.

A simple way to describe Silvie is:

**Silvie is the AI personal assistant that gives every salesperson, consultant and traveling employee their own assistant — keeping every trip, meeting, booking, receipt, invoice, and calendar commitment under control, all in one place.**

The initial product could start by connecting to email and calendar, automatically building a live dashboard of upcoming trips and meetings, detecting missing actions, and enriching calendar events. Over time, Silvie could evolve into a more proactive agent that books restaurants, arranges taxis, suggests travel changes, prepares meeting briefs, submits expenses, and coordinates with internal systems.

Silvie’s long-term vision is to become the default personal assistant for any business team whose people travel: a trusted assistant that understands each user’s schedule, logistics, preferences, business context, and priorities — and quietly closes the loops that busy professionals do not have time to manage themselves.
