# Silvie — AI Personal Assistant for Executives on the Road

**Silvie** is an AI-powered personal assistant app designed for busy executives, sales leaders, VP-level managers, consultants, and frequent business travellers who need help managing the operational complexity of being constantly on the road.

Unlike traditional travel apps, calendar tools, or expense platforms, Silvie is not focused on one narrow task. Instead, it acts as a single command center for everything related to an executive’s schedule, travel, meetings, bookings, logistics, receipts, and follow-ups.

Silvie connects to the user’s email, calendar, travel platforms, expense tools, taxi apps, restaurant booking systems, and CRM where relevant. It automatically detects and organizes flights, hotels, train tickets, restaurant reservations, taxis, meetings, invoices, receipts, and missing actions into one clear mobile-first dashboard.

The core value of Silvie is not simply booking a flight or scheduling a meeting. Its real value is in keeping track of everything that has already been booked, what still needs to be done, what has changed, and what may cause problems. Silvie helps users avoid missed meetings, poor travel planning, missing invoices, forgotten receipts, calendar conflicts, and last-minute logistics issues.

For example, Silvie can identify that a flight has been booked but no hotel is attached to the trip, that a restaurant reservation has not been added to the calendar, that a taxi is needed after landing, or that a customer meeting is too close to the arrival time. It can also enrich calendar events with addresses, travel time, confirmation numbers, booking links, receipts, and relevant notes.

Silvie is best understood as an **AI Chief of Staff for business travel and executive logistics**. It sits above existing tools rather than replacing them. Instead of competing directly with platforms like Concur, Navan, TravelPerk, Outlook, Google Calendar, Uber, or OpenTable, Silvie integrates with them and gives the user a unified, intelligent layer across all of their commitments.

The product is especially valuable for roles where time, reliability, and coordination matter: sales executives, travelling managers, founders, consultants, and senior leaders. These users often have complex days involving flights, hotels, client meetings, internal calls, dinners, taxis, receipts, and follow-up actions. Silvie helps them stay in control without needing a human personal assistant.

A simple way to describe Silvie is:

**Silvie is the AI personal assistant that keeps every trip, meeting, booking, receipt, invoice, and calendar commitment under control — all in one place.**

The initial product could start by connecting to email and calendar, automatically building a live dashboard of upcoming trips and meetings, detecting missing actions, and enriching calendar events. Over time, Silvie could evolve into a more proactive agent that books restaurants, arranges taxis, suggests travel changes, prepares meeting briefs, submits expenses, and coordinates with internal systems.

Silvie’s long-term vision is to become the operating system for executives on the move: a trusted assistant that understands the user’s schedule, logistics, preferences, business context, and priorities — and quietly closes the loops that busy professionals do not have time to manage themselves.
