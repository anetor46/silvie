Find free time slots in the user's calendar (and optionally other people's calendars) within a given window. Use this when the user asks to schedule a meeting, find availability, or check when they (or someone else) is free. Returns both the `busy` ranges and the computed `free` gaps that are at least `min_duration_minutes` long. Prefer this over scanning events with `get_calendar_events` when the goal is to find a slot.
